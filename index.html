About

I'm Manan Kosta, a video editor focused on turning raw footage into something people actually want to watch through to the end.

My work splits between two halves of the same craft: structure — pacing a story so it lands — and look — grading it so it feels intentional, not just "color corrected." Most projects move between Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve in the same session.

Lately that means a lot of short-form: Reels and Shorts that need to hook in the first second, plus longer narrative and brand pieces where the edit has room to breathe.
